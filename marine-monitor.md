# Autonomous Marine Environmental Monitor

## Project Overview
This project focused on developing a physical, biodegradable robot designed for long-term marine environmental monitoring and tracking surface anomalies like oil spills.

## Engineering Challenges & Design
* **Material Selection:** Researched and utilized specific biodegradable polymers capable of surviving high-pressure underwater environments before safely decomposing.
* **Geometric Modeling:** Modeled the chassis using Autodesk Fusion 360, incorporating a custom internal honeycomb structure for buoyancy control and structural integrity.

## Results
The prototype successfully maintained structural stability during pressure testing and successfully broadcast simulated sensor data.
