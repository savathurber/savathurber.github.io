# Renewable Energy Techno-Economic Analysis

## Project Overview
Developed a comprehensive techno-economic proposal for a utility-scale renewable energy facility, evaluating both financial viability and regional grid integration.

## Technical Methodology
* **Financial Modeling:** Calculated operational expenditure (OPEX), internal rate of return (IRR), and payback periods based on localized energy market data.
* **Public Policy & Logistics:** Mapped out permitting frameworks and engineered mitigation strategies for local community impact concerns (NIMBYism).

## Key Takeaway
The completed proposal demonstrated a viable pathway to grid-interconnection with a projected stable IRR over a 20-year asset lifecycle.
